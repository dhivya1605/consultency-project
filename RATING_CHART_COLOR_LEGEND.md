## 🎯 What Each Color Slice Size Means

### **Larger Slice** = More products with that rating
```
If RED slice is 30% → 30% of products have 3.8 star rating
If PURPLE slice is 10% → 10% of products have 4.2 star rating
```

### **Smaller Slice** = Fewer products with that rating
```
If PURPLE slice is 5% → Only 5% of products are excellent
If RED slice is 15% → Only 15% of products are poor
```

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why are there 5 colors?**
A: To represent 5 different rating levels (3.8, 3.9, 4.0, 4.1, 4.2 stars)

**Q: What if a product has 4.5 stars?**
A: It would be grouped into the closest category (4.2 stars/PURPLE)

**Q: Can I change the colors?**
A: Colors are standardized for consistency across reports

**Q: What's the difference between 4.1 and 4.2?**
A: 4.1 (TEAL) = Very Good, 4.2 (PURPLE) = Excellent

**Q: How often does the chart update?**
A: Real-time as new ratings are added
